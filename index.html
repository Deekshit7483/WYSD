About World Youth Skills Day

World Youth Skills Day is celebrated annually on July 15th to highlight the strategic importance of equipping young people with skills for employment, decent work, and entrepreneurship. Our platform provides a comprehensive assessment environment where participants can demonstrate their mathematical abilities, receive detailed feedback, and connect with a global community of young learners committed to excellence and continuous improvement.
